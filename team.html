 Our Leadership Team 




Physician Owned and Operated


We bring our extensive experience as physicians to every aspect of home care





Dr. Ken


CHIC Co-Founder

One of the co-founders of CHIC. He is a Chicago native whose educational history includes Lane Technical High School, University of Illinois Undergraduate and Medical School, and Northwestern Memorial Hospital post graduate residency. Dr. Ken is a Board Certified Internal Medicine physician who has delivered comprehensive medical care in Highland Park, Illinois for almost 25 years. He holds faculty positions at the North Shore University Health System hospital network, as well as the University of Chicago Pritzker School of Medicine. Dr. Ken also has held medical director positions at several rehabilitation facilities, nursing homes, hospice companies, and home health companies.

Dr. Steve


CHIC Co-Founder

One of the co-founders of CHIC and an internal medicine physician in Chicago, IL. In 1989, he received his medical degree from S.I.U. Medical School in Springfield, Il. Dr. Steve did his medical residency training at Northwestern University from 1989-1992 and was chosen to be Chief Resident from 1992-1993. He has held several major teaching positions at Northwestern, receiving the Dean’s Award for Teaching Excellence in 1995. He continues to actively teach at Northwestern and maintains a busy Internal Medicine practice. Dr. Steve’s interests include entrepreneurial and environmental issues. He currently holds four U.S. patents in the renewable energy sector, and he is president and founder of Innovative Technology Advisors.

Katya


Director of Operations

Oversees all company operations for CHIC, including our in home care services, independent senior living community care services, and nursing services. She is our liaison between the CHIC Executive Team, the clients and their families, our caregiver team and our independent senior living communities. Katya has been an administrator in the home health and home care fields for over a decade bringing vast experience and knowledge to our company and our clients. She loves working with seniors and advocating for their wellness and safety in all phases of their independent living. In her spare time she enjoys yoga, fitness, and travel.
