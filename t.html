 Home Care Technology 


 We have the latest state of the art systems and technology to assist clients, their families, caregivers and our management team in achieving the best health outcomes. Our technology keeps wellness information up to date and accessible to give our clients and caregivers powerful tools to increase health, safety, and happiness.


Our Care Platform— We currently use the most sophisticated cloud based/smart phone app platform that allows our caregivers to check in and out, make notes, and send important real time updates about the clients’ status. CHIC’s cloud technology gives caregivers and the management team 24/7 access to all active clients’ schedules, care plans, medication lists, and care notes. Designated family members may similarly access this real time information.


Dementia Care Technology— Optional GPS tracking devices and motion detectors keep our dementia clients safe and can be monitored in real time by family. Devices are used to signal caregivers and family when they wander from their safe environment.


Smartphone Apps— We can direct clients/families to obtaining a host of smartphone apps that promote healthy living habits and track bio information like heart rate, movement, and sleep patterns.
