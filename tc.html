 Traditional Home Care Services 


Comprehensive Services

 We offer an array of home care services administered by trained professionals. Our services can be tailored to every client's unique situation so that they can receive the best care while staying in their home. As an alternative to a hospital or nursing facility setting, home care services are often less expensive and just as effective with the added benefit of keeping the client in a familiar and comfortable environment. In addition to our in home care services we also offer our same services in independent, assisted, and other retirement facilities.


Daily Living Assistance— The activities of daily living (ADLs) include bathing, dressing, transferring, toileting, eating, and walking - those that need assistance with all or some of these activities can schedule daily visits from our care team.


Live - Ins— Our live-in caregivers provide 24 hour assistance with all of the above Daily Living Assistance (ADL) services but designed for those clients requiring prolonged comfort and safety.


Ambulatory Assistance— Assistance with ambulation both inside and outside of the home. Help getting in and out of bed, chairs, bathrooms, showers, and vehicles.


Cooking/Feeding Assistance— We make sure our clients are getting the nutrition they need to live healthy lives. Our home care aides prepare meals according to nutritional needs and assist in eating/drinking.


Exercising Assistance— Keeping fit is an important part of staying physically, mentally, and emotionally healthy. Our care staff can design an exercise program that meets your needs and help you perform them regularly.


Transportation Assistance— We arrange for transportation to and from medical appointments and other common destinations such as the grocery store, shopping mall, and senior center.


Laundry/House Cleaning— We can do laundry and a variety of other light house work understanding that a clean orderly home enhances one’s well-being.
